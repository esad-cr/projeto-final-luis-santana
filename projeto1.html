Game Intro - Strider

This project was designed to serve as a potential intro sequence for a retro-style game emulating the aesthetic of PlayStation 1, Nintendo GameCube, and Xbox titles from the late 90s and early 2000s. It consists of a fixed-camera 3D animation featuring multiple environments and a subject in motion.
